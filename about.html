WHO WE ARE

IITB Racing is the official motorsport team of the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay. Founded in 2007, we are a multidisciplinary student-run team of 70+ members dedicated to designing, building, & competing with formula-style electric race cars.

OUR MISSION

To revolutionize electric mobility in India through hands-on engineering, innovation, and competitive excellence.
