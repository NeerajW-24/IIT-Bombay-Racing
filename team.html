ABOUT TEAM

IIT Bombay Racing is Asia's premier Formula Student team with over 100 dedicated members from diverse engineering and non-engineering backgrounds. Structured into four divisions: Mechanical, Electrical, Driverless (DV), and Organisational, the team combines technical innovation with operational excellence. Guided by experienced leads and collaborative teamwork, we design and build race cars that are lighter, faster, and engineered with precision, performance consistency, and dependability every season.
