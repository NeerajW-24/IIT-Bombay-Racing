Driven by Your Support

The success of the IIT Bombay Racing Team is made possible by our vital partners and community supporters. We are powered by their generous contributions, whether through funding, products, or technical services. Every year, this support enables us to design, build, and compete at the highest levels of student motorsport globally. In gratitude, we provide our partners with extensive publicity on our race car, online, and at events. We are committed to building strong, mutually beneficial relationships and sharing our journey with those who make it possible.
